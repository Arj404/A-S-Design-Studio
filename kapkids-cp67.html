Client Kapkids
Type Children's Retail Store
Year 2024
Location CP67, Chandigarh

About the Project

Kapkids CP67 represents our innovative approach to children's retail design in Chandigarh's vibrant commercial hub. Completed in January 2024, this project showcases how thoughtful design can create an engaging and functional space for both children and parents.

Located in the bustling CP67 area of Chandigarh, the store design focuses on creating a welcoming environment that appeals to young customers while maintaining the sophistication expected in modern retail spaces. The layout optimizes product visibility and customer flow, ensuring an enjoyable shopping experience.

Project Highlights

  • Child-friendly layout and design
  • Vibrant color palette
  • Efficient space planning
  • Modern retail fixtures
  • Brand identity integration
  • Durable materials
