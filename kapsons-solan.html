About the Project

The Kapsons Solan project represents our expertise in creating sophisticated multi-brand retail environments. This flagship store for the Kapsons Group showcases our ability to design spaces that seamlessly integrate multiple premium clothing brands while maintaining a cohesive and luxurious shopping experience.

Located in Solan, Himachal Pradesh, this retail space was designed to reflect the premium positioning of the Kapsons brand while providing an inviting atmosphere for customers. Our design approach focused on creating distinct zones for different brands while ensuring smooth customer flow and optimal product visibility throughout the store.

The project features carefully curated lighting design, premium finishes, and strategic space planning that enhances the shopping experience and encourages customer engagement with the merchandise.

Project Highlights

  • Strategic space planning
