Kapoor Residence, Model Town

Client Kapoor Family
Type Residential Design
Location Model Town, New Delhi
Year 2020
Status Completed

About the Project

The Kapoor Residence in Model Town, Delhi, reflects a rare design balance — minimal yet expressive, modern yet royal. The client's vision was clear: keep the base clean, but let the personality speak through bold choices. Pop colors create identity in each space, warm lighting adds depth and richness, and large-scale furniture enhances the sense of luxury throughout.

Being a joint family residence, the home was thoughtfully planned with each floor dedicated to a nuclear family — maintaining privacy without losing emotional connection. Common areas were designed to encourage interaction, creating individual comfort within a shared home. Each room carries a distinct theme, yet the house feels connected — a seamless experience rather than isolated designs.

Project Highlights

  • Pop color accents for spatial identity
  • Warm lighting for depth and richness
  • Large-scale furniture for a sense of luxury
  • Layered textures across every room
  • Floor-by-floor planning for joint family privacy
  • Seamless flow between distinct room themes
