About the Project

This apartment in Gurgaon was designed to feel both lived-in and beautifully considered — a home where warmth and refinement coexist naturally. The brief centred on creating spaces that support daily life without sacrificing elegance, drawing on a palette of layered textures and carefully chosen materials.

Each room was designed as part of a cohesive whole, with a consistent design language threading through the living areas, bedrooms, and kitchen. Bespoke joinery, curated finishes, and deliberate lighting choices elevate the interiors, resulting in a home that feels personally crafted yet effortlessly timeless.

Project Highlights

  • Full apartment interior design and execution
  • Layered textures with a warm, grounded palette
  • Bespoke joinery and custom built-ins
  • Cohesive design language across all spaces
  • Considered lighting design and fixture selection
  • Refined detailing from floor to ceiling
