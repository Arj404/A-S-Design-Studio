About the Project

This apartment interior in Gurgaon was designed around a single guiding idea: making everyday living feel effortlessly refined. The brief called for a home that felt calm and uncluttered without feeling bare — spaces that are full of personality but never overwhelming.

Every room was approached as a composition — balancing texture, light, and proportion to create a sense of quiet sophistication. Warm neutrals anchor the palette while considered material choices — from wall treatments to joinery — add layers of visual interest. The result is a home that is both liveable and beautiful, where every detail serves the whole.

Project Highlights

  • Contemporary apartment interior design
  • Warm neutral palette with layered textures
  • Custom joinery and bespoke material selections
  • Optimised spatial planning for apartment living
  • Cohesive design language across all rooms
  • Refined finishes with restrained detailing
