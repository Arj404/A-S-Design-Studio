Type Retail Design
Location Delhi
Year 2026

A Salon Designed to Elevate the Experience

This salon interior in Delhi was designed around a single idea: that a great haircut starts well before the first snip. The space had to feel premium yet approachable — a destination in its own right, not merely a functional studio.

Clean lines, a considered material palette, and a carefully orchestrated lighting scheme work together to create an environment that is calm, focused, and unmistakably polished. Bespoke joinery and custom station design ensure every client's experience feels personal and unhurried.

Project Highlights

  • Custom styling station design
  • Layered ambient and task lighting
  • Premium material and finish selections
  • Dedicated reception and waiting area
  • Brand-coherent signage and décor
