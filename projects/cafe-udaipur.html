Cafe, Udaipur

Type Commercial Design
Location Udaipur, Rajasthan
Year 2025

A Cafe Rooted in Udaipur's Character

Nestled in the heart of the City of Lakes, this cafe interior draws inspiration from Udaipur's rich heritage of craftsmanship and its vibrant, sun-drenched palette. The brief called for a space that feels warm and unhurried — a retreat from the city's bustle while remaining unmistakably contemporary.

Layered textures, natural materials, and thoughtful lighting combine to create an atmosphere that is as comfortable for an early morning coffee as it is for an evening gathering. Every detail — from the bespoke joinery to the curated art — reinforces a cohesive identity rooted in place.

Project Highlights

  • Locally inspired material palette
  • Custom joinery and bespoke furniture
  • Zoned seating for varied dining experiences
  • Integrated ambient and accent lighting
  • Brand-coherent visual identity throughout
