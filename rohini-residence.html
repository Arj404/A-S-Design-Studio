About the Project

The Rohini Residence was envisioned as a space that speaks quiet luxury — not loud, not busy, but deeply refined and expansive. The client's vision was clear: create a sense of grandeur without overwhelming the senses. Luxury here is defined by proportion, scale, and restraint — a soft neutral palette with warm undertones, balanced textures, and finishes that feel premium yet calm.

At the heart of the home is a double-height living area — a large screen wall anchors the space as a signature focal point, while a skylight above brings in natural light that shifts mood through the day. Oversized rooms, wide circulation areas, and open yet well-defined common zones give every corner an unrestricted, breathable quality. True luxury, achieved not by adding more, but by knowing what to hold back.

Project Highlights

  • Double-height living area with skylight
  • Large screen wall as a signature focal point
  • Oversized rooms for comfort and flexibility
  • Soft neutral palette with warm undertones
  • Premium finishes with layered textures
  • Proportion-led design for timeless elegance
