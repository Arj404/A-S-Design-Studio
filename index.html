About Us

Creating Timeless Architecture

The A&S Design Studio is a forward-thinking architectural practice dedicated to creating exceptional spaces that blend functionality with aesthetic excellence. Led by Ar. Shreya Jain, we specialize in retail and residential architecture that stands the test of time.

Our approach combines innovative design thinking with practical construction knowledge, ensuring that every project we undertake not only looks stunning but also functions seamlessly for its intended purpose.
