Our Design Process

A systematic approach that ensures every project meets the highest standards of design excellence and client satisfaction.

01

Discovery & Consultation

We begin with an in-depth consultation to understand your vision, requirements, and constraints. This phase includes site analysis, program development, and establishing project goals.

  • Initial client meeting and brief analysis
  • Site survey and feasibility study
  • Budget and timeline establishment
  • Regulatory requirements review
02

Concept Development

Our team develops initial design concepts that respond to your brief while exploring creative possibilities. We present multiple options with sketches, mood boards, and preliminary layouts.

  • Conceptual sketches and diagrams
  • Space planning and layout options
  • Material and finish exploration
  • Client feedback and refinement
03

Design Development

The chosen concept is refined and developed into detailed designs. We create comprehensive drawings, 3D visualizations, and specifications that bring your vision to life.

  • Detailed architectural drawings
  • 3D renderings and visualizations
  • Material specifications and samples
  • Structural and MEP coordination
04

Documentation & Permits

We prepare comprehensive construction documents and handle all regulatory approvals. Our detailed drawings ensure smooth construction and compliance with all building codes.

  • Construction drawings and details
  • Building permit applications
  • Regulatory compliance documentation
  • Contractor bidding support
05

Construction Administration

Throughout construction, we provide ongoing support to ensure the design intent is realized. We conduct site visits, review submittals, and address any issues that arise.

  • Regular site inspections
  • Contractor coordination
  • Quality control and compliance
  • Project completion and handover
